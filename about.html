Born in 2019, somewhere between a prayer and a mistake.

Pray For Me was never meant to be a brand. It started as a notebook — pages filled during nights that lasted too long, in rooms that felt too small.

The name came from a note left on a desk. Nobody remembers who wrote it first.

We make clothes for people who feel too much and show too little. For those who pray in silence and dress like it's their last day.

Every piece is a relic. Every stitch, a small act of survival.
